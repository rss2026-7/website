Our Mission

We are RSS Team 7, a group of MIT students working together in MIT 6.4200 — Robotics: Science and Systems (Spring 2026). Our goal is to design, build, and program an autonomous racecar that can navigate real-world environments using state-of-the-art perception, planning, and control algorithms.

Our Robot

Photo coming after our first lab

Our robot is a 1/10-scale autonomous racecar built on the MIT RACECAR platform. Equipped with a LIDAR sensor, depth camera, and IMU, it will tackle challenges from wall-following to full autonomous navigation. Check back as we document our progress throughout the semester!
